AG App is designed to explore our Music catalogue and buy items.

Instructions:

Select Records item from Menu.

Browse Albums list and select desired record.

Add to Shopping Cart the selected record.

Select another Item by returning to the Records list or go Menu to Check Out

Select Check Out item from menu.

The list of items selected appears.

Remove an element by sliding the row to the left.

Place Order to verify the items to buy and total cost.

Provide the Shipping Information and confirm to place the order.
